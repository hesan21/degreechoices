Progressivism and Academia:
The Most Progressive Universities in the US

What is the most progressive university in the US? It’s a question that’s often asked but rarely answered in any satisfactory way. Although the question of progressivism is open ended and subjective to some extent, we have endeavored to define the most progressive institutions based on a variety of metrics.

Methodology

We surveyed the 30 top-rated universities in the US and assessed their levels of progressivism by focusing on the following:

Diversity

Freedom of Speech

Inclusivity

Health and Wellbeing

Sustainability

These metrics were supplemented by scores relating to health and wellbeing. Data was collected from primary and secondary sources, including university policy pages and external organizations such as STARS, IV Stats, and College Factual. Data was collected in its raw form before being normalized to allow for comparisons. Finally, we categorized the normalized data to allow for easier reading and comparisons. Each university was then given an overall progressiveness rating based on its scores across all categories.
